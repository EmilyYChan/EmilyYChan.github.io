Who We Are


Established in 2001, we are Harvard's one and only mariachi band. The group is completely student run, with newer members learning the craft from older members. Throughout the year, we performs a variety of concerts and gigs, culminating in a year-end trip every spring. Members come from a variety of backgrounds ranging from mariachi to classical to folk, but all share a love of mariachi music.

Book Us


Contact us at mariachi.veritas@gmail.com with your performance date, time, and location to get a quote.

  • Events

    We perform at a variety of events in the greater Boston area, including community gatherings, festivals, parties, and other celebrations.

  • Serenades

    We perform serenades in the Harvard Square area year-round, before and after our regular rehearsals, as well as on Valentine's Day.

  • Payment

    We accept payment in the form of cash, venmo, or check (made out to “Mariachi Veritas de Harvard”). Payment includes cost of transportation.

  • Cancellations

    We reserve the right to cancel an outdoor performance under weather conditions that could be harmful to our instruments, such as heat, cold, or rain.
